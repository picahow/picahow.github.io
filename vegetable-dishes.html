Sabah Vegetables

A refreshing and healthy dish that showcases the fresh produce of Sabah. Similar to Chinese vegetables found in Hong Kong, the vibrant greens and light seasoning make it a perfect side dish that complements any meal. I love its simplicity and how it highlights the natural flavors of the vegetables.

Stir-Fried Kangkung (Water Spinach)

A simple yet incredibly flavorful dish made with water spinach (kangkung) stir-fried with garlic, chili, and sometimes fermented shrimp paste (belacan). It’s one of my favorite vegetable dishes because it’s vibrant, spicy, and full of umami. The tender stems and soft leaves make it light yet satisfying, and it pairs wonderfully with rice or other dishes.

Sayur Lodeh

A comforting and flavorful vegetable curry dish that’s one of my favorite ways to enjoy vegetables. Made with a mix of vegetables like cabbage, carrots, long beans, and tofu, cooked in a creamy, spiced coconut milk broth, it’s rich and satisfying. I love how the mild spices and coconut milk create a warming, hearty dish that pairs perfectly with rice.
