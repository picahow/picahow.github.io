Ais Kacang

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shaved ice
  • 1/4 cup red beans
  • 1/4 cup sweet corn
  • 1/4 cup grass jelly (cincau)
  • Syrups (rose syrup or palm sugar syrup)
  • Evaporated milk
  • 1 scoop any flavour of ice cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, layer the red beans, sweet corn, and grass jelly.
  2. Top with a generous mound of shaved ice.
  3. Drizzle with syrups and evaporated milk to taste.
  4. Serve immediately as a refreshing dessert on a hot day.
