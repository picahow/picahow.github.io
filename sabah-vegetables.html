Sabah Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch of Sabah vegetables (fern, water spinach, or similar greens)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and trim the vegetables. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sautÃ© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Season with oyster sauce and salt, and cook until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  5. Serve immediately as a side dish or with steamed rice.
