Teh Tarik

Malaysia’s beloved “pulled tea” that I can’t get enough of. The creamy and frothy texture, paired with the perfect balance of tea and sweetness, makes it a comforting drink. It’s a must-have for me, especially during breakfast or late-night chats.

Sirap Bandung

A sweet and creamy rose-flavored drink that’s a favorite of mine. It’s made with rose syrup and condensed milk, often served chilled with ice. I love the floral aroma and the pretty pink color, which makes it so visually appealing. The sweetness and creaminess are incredibly indulgent, and it’s the perfect drink to pair with spicy dishes or enjoy on its own.
