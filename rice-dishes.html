Nasi Lemak

One of my favourite classic Malaysian dishes. A fragrant rice dish cooked in coconut milk, served with sambal, fried crispy anchovies, peanuts, and boiled egg.

Beef Rendang

An iconic Malaysian dish that’s rich, tender, and bursting with flavor. The slow-cooked beef in creamy coconut milk and aromatic spices makes every bite heavenly. It’s the ultimate comfort food, especially when paired with steamed rice.

Hainanese Chicken Rice

One of my all-time favorite comfort foods. This dish features tender poached chicken served with fragrant rice cooked in chicken stock, accompanied by a flavorful chili sauce, ginger paste, and dark soy sauce. I love how the simplicity of the ingredients comes together to create something so satisfying. The balance of the juicy chicken, aromatic rice, and tangy sauces makes it a meal I can’t resist.

Nasi Goreng

A hearty and flavorful Indonesian fried rice dish that always hits the spot. It’s stir-fried with a mix of kecap manis (sweet soy sauce), garlic, shallots, and sometimes shrimp or chicken, topped with a fried egg. I adore the smoky, savory-sweet taste of the rice, especially when paired with crispy prawn crackers and spicy sambal. It’s a dish that’s perfect for any time of the day.
