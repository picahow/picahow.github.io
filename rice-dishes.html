Nasi Lemak

One of my favourite classic Malaysian dishes. A fragrant rice dish cooked in coconut milk, served with sambal, fried crispy anchovies, peanuts, and boiled egg.

Beef Rendang

An iconic Malaysian dish that’s rich, tender, and bursting with flavor. The slow-cooked beef in creamy coconut milk and aromatic spices makes every bite heavenly. It’s the ultimate comfort food, especially when paired with steamed rice.
