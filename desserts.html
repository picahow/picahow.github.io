Ais Kacang

A colorful and refreshing dessert that is perfect for a hot day. The combination of shaved ice, sweet syrups, jelly, red beans, and evaporated milk is pure delight, (along with optional ice cream!). It is a nostalgic treat that my grandparents would buy for me back in Malaysia.

Kuih Lapis

A colorful and delightful layered steamed cake that’s as fun to eat as it is to look at. Made from rice flour, coconut milk, and sugar, the layers are soft, chewy, and slightly sweet. I love peeling and eating the layers one by one. The subtle coconut flavor is comforting and satisfying.

Bubur Cha Cha

A deliciously sweet and creamy Nyonya dessert that I can never say no to. Made with a medley of sweet potatoes, taro, and tapioca pearls cooked in coconut milk with pandan and sugar, it’s a warm, comforting bowl of goodness. I love how the creamy coconut milk blends with the earthy sweetness of the root vegetables, creating a dessert that feels both indulgent and wholesome.
