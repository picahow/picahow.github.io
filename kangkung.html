Stir-Fried Kangkung (Water Spinach)

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch kangkung (water spinach)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon sambal belacan
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a wok and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add sambal belacan and stir-fry for 1 minute.
  3. Add the kangkung and soy sauce, stirring until the leaves wilt.
  4. Serve hot as a flavorful vegetable side dish.
