Roti Canai

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and salt in a bowl. Gradually add water, condensed milk, and melted butter, kneading until a soft dough forms.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls and coat with oil. Let rest for 2 hours.
  3. Flatten each ball into a thin sheet, then fold into a square or coil it into a round shape.
  4. Fry on a hot griddle with a bit of oil until golden brown on both sides. Serve with dhal curry or sugar.
