Teh Tarik

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons black tea leaves
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 2 tablespoons condensed milk

Instructions

  1. Add tea leaves to boiling water and steep for 3 minutes.
  2. Strain the tea into a cup and stir in condensed milk.
  3. To achieve the signature frothy texture, pour the tea back and forth between two cups several times from a height.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
