Hainanese Chicken Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 1 whole chicken
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 slices of ginger
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • Soy sauce for serving
  • Chili sauce for serving

Instructions

  1. Boil the chicken with garlic and ginger slices until cooked. Set aside the broth.
  2. Cook the rice using the chicken broth for extra flavor.
  3. Rub the cooked chicken with sesame oil and slice it into pieces.
  4. Serve the chicken over a bed of fragrant rice with soy sauce and chili sauce on the side.
