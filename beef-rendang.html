Beef Rendang

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef (cubed)
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 3 tablespoons rendang paste
  • 2 stalks of lemongrass
  • 6 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric powder
  • 1-2 cups of rice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot and fry the rendang paste until fragrant.
  2. Add beef cubes and stir to coat with the paste.
  3. Pour in coconut milk and add lemongrass, turmeric powder, and lime leaves.
  4. Simmer on low heat until the beef is tender and the sauce thickens.
  5. Serve with steamed rice or lemang.
