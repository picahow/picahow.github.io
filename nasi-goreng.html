Nasi Goreng

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sambal or chili paste
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Vegetables of your choice (carrots, peas, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a wok and scramble the eggs. Remove and set aside.
  2. Fry minced garlic and sambal until fragrant. Add vegetables and stir-fry.
  3. Add the cooked rice and soy sauce, mixing well.
  4. Return the scrambled eggs to the wok and stir everything together. Serve hot.
