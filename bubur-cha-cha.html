Bubur Cha Cha

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yam, diced
  • 1 cup sweet potato, diced
  • 1/2 cup tapioca pearls
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup sugar

Instructions

  1. Boil yam, sweet potato, and tapioca pearls separately until tender. Set aside.
  2. In a pot, combine coconut milk, water, and sugar. Heat gently but do not boil.
  3. Add the cooked yam, sweet potato, and tapioca pearls to the coconut milk mixture.
  4. Serve warm or chilled as a creamy dessert.
