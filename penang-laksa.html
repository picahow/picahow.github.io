Penang Laksa

Ingredients

  • 500g mackerel fish
  • 6 cups of water
  • 1 packet of laksa noodles
  • 2 stalks of lemongrass
  • 1 cup tamarind juice
  • 1 tablespoon chili paste
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Sliced cucumber and pineapple

Instructions

  1. Boil the mackerel in water until cooked, then remove and flake the fish meat.
  2. Blend the fish meat into a paste and return to the pot with tamarind juice.
  3. Add lemongrass, chili paste, and season with salt. Let it simmer.
  4. Cook laksa noodles according to package instructions.
  5. Serve noodles in a bowl, pour the soup over, and garnish with mint leaves, cucumber, and pineapple slices.
