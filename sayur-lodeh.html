Sayur Lodeh

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cabbage, chopped
  • 1 cup long beans, chopped
  • 1 cup carrots, julienned
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add curry powder and stir for 1 minute.
  3. Add coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add vegetables and cook until tender. Season with salt and serve with rice.
