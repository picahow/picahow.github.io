Satay

A Malaysian classic that’s always a crowd-pleaser. The tender, marinated meat skewers grilled to perfection and served with rich peanut sauce are absolutely delicious. It’s one of my favorite dishes to enjoy during gatherings or celebrations.

Roti Canai

A flaky and crispy Malaysian flatbread that I absolutely love. It’s golden and buttery on the outside, soft and slightly chewy on the inside, and is typically served with a side of curry or dhal for dipping. I enjoy the contrast of textures and the satisfaction of tearing apart each layer. It’s a breakfast favorite that’s comforting, flavorful, and perfect with a cup of tea.

Keropok Lekor

A savory and addictive Malaysian fish snack made from a mix of fish and sago flour, deep-fried to golden perfection. These crunchy or chewy fish crackers are best enjoyed with a sweet and spicy chili dipping sauce. I love the rich, umami flavor of the fish, and the combination of textures makes it an irresistible snack that’s hard to stop eating.
