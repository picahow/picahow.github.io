Char Kway Teow

Ingredients

  • 200g flat rice noodles
  • 100g shrimp, peeled
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili paste
  • Bean sprouts
  • Chives

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a wok and stir-fry garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add shrimp and cook until pink, then push them aside.
  3. Crack in the eggs and scramble lightly.
  4. Add the noodles, soy sauce, and chili paste, stirring well.
  5. Toss in bean sprouts and chives, cooking for another minute. Serve hot.
