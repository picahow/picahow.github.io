Keropok Lekor (Fish Crackers)

Ingredients

  • 500g fish fillet (mackerel or sardines)
  • 300g tapioca flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Oil for frying
  • Sambal or chili sauce for dipping

Instructions

  1. Blend the fish fillet until it forms a paste.
  2. Mix the paste with tapioca flour, salt, and sugar to form a dough.
  3. Roll the dough into long, thin cylinders.
  4. Boil the cylinders in hot water until they float, then let cool.
  5. Slice the boiled rolls into smaller pieces and deep-fry until golden. Serve with sambal or chili sauce.
