Satay

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, beef or lamb, cut into small cubes
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric powder
  • 2 tablespoons coconut milk
  • Wooden skewers
  • Peanut sauce for dipping

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix curry powder, turmeric powder, and coconut milk. Add the meat and marinate for 2 hours.
  2. Soak wooden skewers in water to prevent burning.
  3. Thread the marinated meat onto the skewers.
  4. Grill the skewers over medium heat, turning occasionally, until the meat is cooked and slightly charred.
  5. Serve hot with peanut sauce and cucumber slices on the side.
