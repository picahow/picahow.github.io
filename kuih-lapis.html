Kuih Lapis

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup tapioca flour
  • 1 1/2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • Food coloring (red or green)

Instructions

  1. Mix rice flour, tapioca flour, coconut milk, water, and sugar until smooth.
  2. Divide the batter into two portions and color one portion with food coloring.
  3. Steam one layer of the batter in a tray for 5 minutes, then add the next layer of the other color. Repeat until all batter is used.
  4. Let cool completely before slicing and serving.
