Mee Goreng

Ingredients

  • 200g yellow noodles
  • 100g tofu, cubed and fried
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Vegetables of your choice (cabbage, carrots, etc.)
  • Lime wedges for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a wok and scramble the eggs. Remove and set aside.
  2. Add the vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  3. Add the noodles, chili sauce, and soy sauce, stirring well.
  4. Toss in the fried tofu and eggs, mixing everything together.
  5. Serve hot with lime wedges on the side.
