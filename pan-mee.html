Pan Mee

Ingredients

  • 500g pan mee noodles
  • 1 cup minced pork
  • 1 cup ikan bilis (anchovies)
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 bunch spinach
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Fry ikan bilis until crispy and set aside.
  2. Cook minced pork in a pan with soy sauce and sesame oil.
  3. Boil chicken stock and add pan mee noodles.
  4. Add spinach and cook until tender.
  5. Serve noodles in a bowl, topped with minced pork and crispy ikan bilis.
